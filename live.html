For Elite Living!
Featuring spectacular luxury residential apartments with modern architecture. Best residential amenities are beyond the standard and all properties are wisely designed using high-quality material and spacious spaces to maximize your comfort. Find your dream apartment exclusively created by one of the US leading estate agents. Buy a prestigious lifestyle with uncompromising luxury for elite living, we focus on details to make people feel comfortable at home. PTC offers premium residential options and lavish properties for people who want to uplift their lifestyle.
Ultra-luxurious life
Rise offers residents one-to-five bedroom spacious residences with high ceilings, floor-to-ceiling glass, and premium finishes. Ten luxurious Tower Suites and seven luxurious penthouses crown the top of the tower.
Rise Features
Modern Design
A modern palette of neutral colors offers an optimal canvas for a range of design aesthetics.
cook and congregate
Italian cabinetry and quartz-stone countertops dress the generously sized kitchens, offering buyers the option of two finish packages that carry throughout the unit.
Private Spas
Well-appointed bathrooms offer freedom of movement and tranquility: a continuation of stone flooring, mosaic backsplash with luxurious details by Danze, and integrated LED mirrors.
peaceful nights
Each master bedroom offers generous space with attached en-suite facilities, providing the much-needed sanctuary an urban resident deserves.
Generous Guest Spaces
Ample living areas offer a pleasant experience for all residents. Powder rooms in most residences allow for en suites in many secondary spaces.
Outdoor Oasis
City-view terraces with glass and aluminum railings are directly accessible from the living room and most bedrooms.
Exclusive Address
Rising above Brickell City Centre, a 500,000-square-foot lifestyle center in the heart of Miami, residents of Rise will enjoy a serene escape and the energy of the most exciting address in Miami.
Envied luxury
Richly designed amenity deck and premium landscaping blend interior and exterior spaces into a warm, elegant mix of offerings to help you enjoy the Miami sunshine.
Building Amenities
Lobby
Once inside, a stunning lobby featuring a bespoke chandelier installation and modern furnishings adorn the comfortable, attended entry.
porte cochère
Arrival into the lavishly detailed porte cochère off of SW 7th Street welcomes guests into Rise, where they are greeted by a 24-hour valet.
Concierge and gallery
On-site concierge is available to residents 7 days a week. The gallery is ideal for socializing with family and guests or relaxing while listening to curated playlists.
Library
A spacious library with reading nooks that may provide inspiration. Featuring sleek, eye-catching architecture and thoughtful design accents.
Fête
Communal suites, with living and dining spaces, mean it’s never been easier – or more enjoyable – to entertain at home, with a chefworthy kitchen perfect for all of life's celebrations.
Hammam Spa
Lap up the lifestyle and unwind at the exclusive resident’s spa with shared hammam featuring dipping pools, showers, and steam rooms. An adjacent salon offers even more opportunities for pampering.
Tea lounge
The Tea Lounge creates a light and bright transition to blend indoor and outdoor space — perfect for escaping the heat or some relaxation any time of day.
Work Out
Work up a sweat in our state-of-the-art fitness center, equipped with top-of-the-line resistance and cardio training equipment, and a full-size fitness studio.
Children's Playroom
A modern and tech-savvy playroom offers the perfect diversion for children of all ages.
